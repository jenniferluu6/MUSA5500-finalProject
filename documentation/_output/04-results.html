9 Limitations of This Analysis

9.1 Data Limitations

  1. No Weather Context:
    • Cannot determine if diversions were weather-related
    • BTS data lacks concurrent weather conditions
    • Impact: Cannot verify our assumptions about causation
  2. Single Diversion Only:
    • BTS only records first diversion
    • Cascading diversions not tracked
    • Impact: May underestimate true system disruption
  3. No Diversion Reason:
    • Cannot distinguish: weather vs. mechanical vs. ATC
    • Impact: Cannot identify root causes
  4. U.S. Domestic Only:
    • International diversions excluded
    • Impact: Incomplete picture of some carriers

9.2 Methodological Limitations

  1. K-Means Assumptions:
    • Assumes spherical clusters
    • May miss irregular patterns
    • Mitigation: Cluster results validated visually on maps
  2. Geographic Clustering:
    • Assumes nearby airports are related
    • May miss causal relationships
    • Mitigation: Temporal clustering helps address this
  3. Time Periods:
    • Dataset spans 45 months but not comprehensive
    • Some recent events (post-Dec 2024) not included
    • Impact: Patterns may have shifted since dataset ends

10 Future Research Directions

10.1 Short Term (Easy to Do)

  1. Add Weather Data
    • Join BTS diversions with NOAA weather data
    • Validate weather-related diversion hypothesis
    • Identify weather-sensitive airports
  2. Track Cascading Effects
    • Follow diversions through secondary flights
    • Quantify passenger impact more fully
    • Model cascade duration
  3. Airline Strategy Analysis
    • Compare routing strategies between carriers
    • Identify best practices for diversion handling
    • Benchmark airline performance

10.2 Medium Term (More Work)

  1. Predictive Modeling
    • Use scikit-learn to predict diversion likelihood
    • Input: Route, date, season, weather forecast
    • Output: Diversion probability
  2. Network Analysis
    • Model aviation system as network
    • Identify critical nodes (airports)
    • Analyze network resilience
  3. Economic Impact
    • Estimate costs of diversions to airlines
    • Calculate passenger impact (delays, missed connections)
    • Quantify systemic efficiency loss

10.3 Long Term (Ambitious)

  1. Policy Recommendation Engine
    • Use ML to recommend infrastructure investments
    • Identify bottleneck airports needing capacity
    • Model impact of policy changes
  2. Real-Time Diversion Forecasting
    • Integrate real-time weather and flight data
    • Predict diversions 24-48 hours in advance
    • Alert airlines and passengers proactively
